Jack Horoho

Owner

Jack is the owner of the Cooking Cabin. He graduated from Purdue University in 2020 with a major in Business Management. When the covid-19 pandemic hit, Jack returned to live with his parents, where he spent his time helping his mother cook her delicious Italian meals with recipes that have been passed down from generation to generation. Jack's parents owned the cooking cabin, and once Jack started to get involved, passed ownership down to him. Jack found himself passionate about cooking, and decided that the rest of the world needed to try his mother's delicious dishes, therefore expanding his parent's business. Some of Jack's favorite dishes include:

  1. Pesto Gnocchi
  2. Homemade Tomato Soup
  3. Penne a la Vodka
